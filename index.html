Navigating Complex Regulations

The India Bazaar project was a massive undertaking requiring over a year of dedicated effort. It involved modeling a complete complex encompassing a hotel, retail spaces, restaurants, and the main bazaar itself.

Our team's deep knowledge of local building codes and proactive liaisoning with government bodies were crucial in overcoming significant hurdles.

Key Challenges

  • 1
    Complex Floor Levels:

    Coordinating varying floor elevations throughout the interconnected design.

  • 2
    Rigorous Compliance:

    Strict adherence to city material calculations necessitated major revisions to elevations and plans.

  • 3
    Site Plan Approvals:

    Securing approval amidst numerous restrictions required persistent iteration.
